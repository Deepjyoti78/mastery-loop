### 1. The Hook: Dashboard (0:00 - 1:00)
**Screen:** `LandingPage` (Dashboard)
*   **Narrative:** "Most EdTech platforms simply dump content on students. MasteryLoop is different. It is an **Operating System for High Performance**."
*   **Action:** 
    *   Show the beautiful **Glassmorphism UI**.
    *   Point to **"Adaptive Insight"** card (Yellow card at bottom). 
    *   *Say:* "The system has already detected I'm weak in 'Pointer Reversal' based on my last quiz. It doesn't just track grades; it tracks cognitive bottlenecks."

### 2. The Core Innovation: Career Architecture (1:00 - 3:00) **(STAR FEATURE)**
**Screen:** Click `Career` (Sidebar)
*   **Narrative:** "Students are often lost. We built the world's first **Career Control Panel**."
*   **Action:**
    1.  Click **Reset Plan** (Top right).
    2.  **Upload a PDF Resume** (Drag & Drop).
    3.  *Say:* "Watch as the AI extracts my profile layer-by-layer." (Wait for text to appear).
    4.  Click **"Run Analysis"**.
    5.  **Show the Dashboard:**
        *   **Readiness Score:** "I'm only 42% ready for a Senior Role."
        *   **Strategy Controls:** Move the slider. "I can simulate a 3-month vs 6-month sprint."
        *   **Critical Gaps (Interactive):** Click a Red Gap card. *Say:* "It doesn't just say 'System Design'. It tells me specifically I'm missing 'Distributed Counters' evidence."
    *   **Micro-Closing:** "This turns vague career anxiety into a tactical execution stack."

### 3. Execution: Academic & Learning (3:00 - 4:30)
**Screen:** Click `Academic` -> Select `Operating Systems`
*   **Narrative:** "Knowing the gap is step one. Closing it is step two."
*   **Action:**
    1.  Show the **Knowledge Graph** (The Flow nodes).
    2.  Click **"Start Module"** or **"View Learning"**.
    3.  **Trigger the Quiz:** Show the **"Mastery Checkpoint"**.
    4.  *Interactive Demo:* Intentionally get a question **WRONG**.
    5.  *Say:* "Notice I failed. The system now enters 'Adaptive Review Mode'. It won't let me proceed until I master this specific micro-concept." (Show the loop).

### 4. Competitive Edge (4:30 - 5:00)
**Screen:** Click `Competitive`
*   **Narrative:** "For students targeting GATE/Google/ISRO, speed matters."
*   **Action:** 
    *   Show the **Speed Drill** card.
    *   Briefly click **Start Drill**.
    *   *Say:* "This isolates specific patterns for high-frequency training."

### 5. Closing (5:00 - 5:30)
